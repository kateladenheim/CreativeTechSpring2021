project 1 // the web as rube goldberg machine

Teammates: Melody & Tao

In the diagram above, we propose a convoluted system to play a 'song of the day,' taking a user on a journey through some of the data available about that day. We begin by asking them abstractly (via emoji) about their feelings, and then slowly populating the date, weather conditions, popular news stories, and whether or not Mercury is in retrograde. We end by presenting them with an automatically generated Haiku and a song, selected based on data from previous queries.
